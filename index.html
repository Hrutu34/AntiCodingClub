We Code Less,
Do More...!

Welcome to the space of progressive minds, where things are created with the touch of creativity and curiosity.
We, at Anti Coding Club, thrive hard to deliver and show the world some of the stunning new ideas that pops up in young innovative minds and that too, with the help of technology

So, How we do that?

1

Planning out!

Our members sit together and decide who will take what task. Each task is based on any problem statement. We ensure that everyone gets a task totally different from others and also the one member for it is interested for the same!  
2

Building up!

Once the planning is done, 2 dates are fixed. One, the Start day and the other one as End day. There will be a time period of around 2 weeks between these 2 days. Completing the undertaken task withing these time period will be challenge for our members. Within this time, they need to create it, test it and if possible, deploy it on any open source or web platforms.
3

Finishing it!

So on the End day, while will likely be on Sunday, we will be going through each and every members work progress and will create a summary of their efforts. All their task will be showcased here on this site`s projects page where our viewers can see them anytime!

How can you know
what we are upto?

Well its quite simple! All you have to do is to go to our What`s New? page and there you are! You can see the countdown for the upcoming End day and can also see which member of ours is working on which task or project. You can also support them with your ideas by commenting on their project in the forum or by simply contacting them...
